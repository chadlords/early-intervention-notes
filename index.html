1. Introduction

Early Intervention Notes ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of the professionals who use our app and the families they serve. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the Early Intervention Notes iOS application.

The app is designed exclusively for licensed early intervention professionals (such as Physical Therapists) to document therapy sessions for children participating in Pennsylvania’s Early Intervention program.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Provider profile information (name, service type, Local ID, manager email)
  • Service Coordinator names
  • Parent/guardian information (name, phone, address, email)
  • Child information (name, date of birth, Local ID, goals, session dates)
  • Session documentation including handwritten notes, targets/outcomes, family plans, and signatures created with Apple Pencil

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information necessary for app functionality
  • iCloud/CloudKit sync data (stored privately in your personal iCloud account)
  • Timestamps and session metadata

We do not collect any information directly from children. All data is entered by licensed professionals.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Enable you to create, edit, and manage therapy session notes
  • Generate professional PDF reports for parents, service coordinators, and managers
  • Support bulk monthly reporting via password-protected ZIP exports
  • Provide data persistence and synchronization across your devices via iCloud
  • Improve the app experience and fix technical issues

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is used by adults to document services for children ages 0–3 participating in Early Intervention programs. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. All data about children is entered and controlled by licensed professionals.

We comply with applicable children’s privacy laws, including COPPA, to the extent they apply to professional documentation tools. Parents or guardians may request access to or deletion of their child’s information through the professional using the app.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data. Information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service coordinators and program managers via PDF or ZIP exports (as directed by you)
  • With parents/guardians via emailed PDF reports
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect rights and safety
  • With Apple’s iCloud service for private data synchronization (you control your iCloud account)

6. Data Security and Retention

We use industry-standard security measures including Apple’s secure iCloud infrastructure and on-device encryption. Handwritten notes created with PencilKit are stored securely on your device and in your private iCloud.

Data is retained as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by your professional obligations and applicable regulations. You can delete individual sessions or entire child records at any time within the app.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete data within the app
  • Export your data via PDF or ZIP
  • Request deletion of your account/data by contacting us
  • Control iCloud sync through your device settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, through the app.
