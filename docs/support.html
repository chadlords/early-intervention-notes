Getting Started

1. Create your Provider profile with your Local ID prefix - 3-digit prefix for case Local IDs.

2. Add children, parents/guardians, and service coordinators.

3. Start a new session and fill out the digital form and/or use Apple Pencil to write directly on the form.

4. Save and export PDFs or monthly ZIP reports as needed. Please make certain you have configured your mail client to send emails from your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud account. The developer has no access to your data.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. You can create and edit sessions completely offline. Changes will sync via iCloud when you're back online.

Parents / Guardians - how or why am I managing their information in this app?

One of the features for this app is to send an email copy of the completed form to the parents or guardians of the child for a particular case. You can access a complete listing of all parents / guardians you have worked with by tapping on the Config gear at the Home Screen, then tapping on the Parents/Guardians tab. You can add / edit / delete records here. When working a particular case, tap on the Parents / Guardians navigation link on the left of the Child Info screen. If any parents or guardians are linked, they will be displayed. Unlinking is done by swiping left, clicking the red "Delete" button to delete the relationship. This will not delete the parent.
If there are no linked parents / guardians, you can use the buttons provided to either add new, or link existing. Adding a new record will not check for existing records, so if a parent seems familiar, you can click to link existing and search your listing. A button to add new will appear at the bottom of the search results if you cannot locate an existing record.

How do I send session notes to a parent / guardian or export reports to my manager?

First, ensure you have configured your Mail Account on your device. From any session or child record, tap the email button to generate a zip archive containing the session note PDF and open the mail interface. Please use the encryption setting for each case, located in the Child Info screen, to ensure you are protecting potentially sensitive and private information. For monthly reporting, navigate to the Export screen (click the gear, then click Export) use this feature to create a password-protected ZIP file. The Export interface will provide a To/From date range selector to generate and zip PDFs for the export.

Can I use this on multiple devices?

Yes. This app uses iCloud sync. Your data will appear on all devices signed into the same Apple ID.

How do I set up my Local ID Prefix?

Upon first use, you enter your Local ID Prefix. Additionally, you can edit the prefix in the Account tab of the app configuration screen. It will be concatenated with the 5-digit number assigned to each case.

How do I manage goals for each case?

Under the Child Info screen, you will find Goals. The goals are a reference point you will use as your provide services, and you will use their number when billing. When you add goals, the goal will automatically receive a number, such as "#5 - ...". It will start at #1, and will count up. Other therapists may be involved and handling some numbers, so you may have #1, #2, #5, #6. Adding a new goal from here would create #7. You can mark goals completed by swiping right, clicking the green "Complete" button. You can also delete goals by swiping left, clicking the red "Delete" button. Editing a goal is as easy as tapping it and changing the text. The goals are not directly placed onto the form for the session as of yet, but mainly for your reference for billing.

What is the deal with the Tracking Recurring Schedule section in the Child Info screen?

Under the Child Info screen, you will find Tracking Recurring Schedule options for each case. The purpose of this little nugget is to help you in a number of ways. First, it will help to fill in the Time-In & Time-Out fields in the form. Second, it will help with filling in the Next Session date and time in the form. Third, it allows you to filter your Home screen to give you your day at-a-glance. Future versions will include a scheduling assistant.
